Surana Strategy and Service

Surana College is a renowned educational institution situated in Bangalore, India. Established in 1995, it is affiliated with Bangalore University and offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in arts, science, commerce, and management. The college has a sprawling campus equipped with modern facilities, including well-furnished classrooms, laboratories, a library, and sports facilities. It boasts a team of experienced faculty members who focus on providing quality education and practical knowledge to students.

In addition to its academic offerings, Surana College emphasizes the overall development of its students through various co-curricular activities. The college organizes seminars, workshops, cultural events, and sports competitions to nurture the talents and skills of its students. It also has a dedicated placement cell that assists students in securing job placements by organizing campus recruitment drives and providing career guidance and training. With its focus on academic excellence, well-rounded development, and strong industry connections, Surana College has established itself as a reputable institution in Bangalore. Its commitment to providing quality education and preparing students for future careers has contributed to its popularity among aspiring students in the region.

When applying to a college, you will typically need to provide several personal details as part of the application process. These details include your full name, date of birth, contact information (address, phone number, and email address), educational background (school/college names, dates of attendance, degrees/certifications earned), citizenship/nationality, and identification documents (such as a passport or national ID card). Additionally, you may be required to submit academic transcripts, a personal statement or essay, and letters of recommendation. These details help the college assess your eligibility, academic background, and personal qualities as they evaluate your application for admission. It's essential to review the specific requirements of each college you're applying to, as they may have additional or slightly different requirements.
