More About Surana

The institution prepares students into self-reliant, independent thinking, globally relevant, skilled and socially useful citizens of the nation embedded with values to contribute in their own mite to the society. Students at the campus will have the due freedom coupled with responsibility and discipline. The college encourages research to challenge the life and knowledge concepts by out-of-box and lateral thinking.

“Student involvement is in the key functioning at the college even in extension activities, community services and sustenance of academic environment.”

Surana College

Surana College, Kengeri Campus, Bangalore, has made a name for itself in both indoor & outdoor sports fields like, Badminton, Hockey, Basketball, Swimming, Volleyball, Chess, Cricket, Football, Handball, Judo, Kabaddi, Netball, Powerlifting, Tennis, Table Tennis, Throwball, Softball, Yoga, Wrestling and Weightlifting. The institution supports and encourages students to involve in these sports. We are ranked at the top by Bangalore University for securing highest points in Sports Competitions/tournaments.

The student community comes from diversified backgrounds, and celebrating our differences, as well as our common interests, helps us unite and learn from each other. We understand the importance of cultural sensitivity in today’s business world and the cultural committee makes the students sensitive to each other’s culture. The Cultural committee is an attempt to capture the vibrant persona of the students by offering them a platform to showcase their hidden talents as musicians, dancers, actors, painters, writers, photographers and dreamers.
